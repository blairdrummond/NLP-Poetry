title : "Ladybird, ladybird,"

author : Mother Goose

text : 
Ladybird, ladybird, 
Fly away home, 
Your house is on fire 
And your children all gone; 
All except one 
And that's little Ann, 
And she has crept under 
The warming pan.




subjects : Nature, Animals, Mythology & Folklore, Fairy-tales & Legends
