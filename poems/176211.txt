title : The Reason

author : Stevie Smith

text : 
My life is vile 
         I hate it so 
         I’ll wait awhile   
         And then I’ll go. 

         Why wait at all? 
         Hope springs alive, 
         Good may befall   
         I yet may thrive. 

It is because I can’t make up my mind   
If God is good, impotent or unkind.




subjects : God & the Divine, Disappointment & Failure, Religion, Faith & Doubt
