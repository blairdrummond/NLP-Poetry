The Pilgrim
John Bunyan
1628-1688
17th Century
England
Religion, Activities, Travels & Journeys, God & the Divine
Poems for Weddings

Rhymed Stanza

Who would true Valour see 
Let him come hither;   
One here will Constant be,   
Come Wind, come Weather.   
There's no Discouragement, 
Shall make him once Relent, 
His first avow'd Intent, 
To be a Pilgrim
