title : Indian Chant

author : Diane Glancy

text : 
Hunted and sung 
unhunted / unsung 

clump of 
loghouse / chaxed hill 

unuttered / unstrung 

clistered bow 
hunted and unsung 

hunted / strung 
hunted / sung.
