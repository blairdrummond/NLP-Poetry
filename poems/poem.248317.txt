The Cherry Trees 
Edward Thomas 
1878-1917

England
Love, Heartache & Loss, Relationships, Men & Women, Nature, Spring, Trees & Flowers, Social Commentaries, Town & Country Life, War & Conflict

Elegy, Quatrain, Rhymed Stanza

The cherry trees bend over and are shedding
On the old road where all that passed are dead,
Their petals, strewing the grass as for a wedding
This early May morn when there is none to wed.
