title : Tradition

author : Lorine Niedecker

text : 
I 
The chemist creates 
      the brazen 
      approximation: 
Life 
      Thy will be done 
      Sun 


II 
Time to garden 
      before I 
      die— 
to meet 
      my compost maker 
      the caretaker 
of the cemetery




subjects : Arts & Sciences, Living, Death, Humor & Satire
