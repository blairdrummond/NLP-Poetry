Little Elegy
Elinor Wylie
1885-1928
Modern

Living, Sorrow & Grieving, Death
Funerals, Farewells & Good Luck, Poems for Weddings

Elegy, Couplet

Withouten you 
No rose can grow; 
No leaf be green 
If never seen 
Your sweetest face; 
No bird have grace 
Or power to sing; 
Or anything 
Be kind, or fair, 
And you nowhere.
