title : 8 count

author : Charles Bukowski

text : 
from my bed 
I watch 
3 birds 
on a telephone   
wire. 

one flies 
off. 
then   
another. 

one is left, 
then 
it too 
is gone. 

my typewriter is 
tombstone 
still. 

and I am 
reduced to bird 
watching. 

just thought I'd 
let you 
know, 
fucker.




subjects : Relationships, Friends & Enemies, Nature, Animals, Arts & Sciences, Humor & Satire
