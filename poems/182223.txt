title : The Crow

author : Kaelum Poulson

text : 
So beautiful   
but often unseen   
a maid of nature   
the street cleaner that’s everywhere   
never thanked   
never liked   
always ignored   
so elegant in a way no one sees   
but without it we would   
be in trash up to our knees   
with the heart of a lion   
the mind of a fox   
the color of the night sky   
a crow   
the unpaid workman   
that helps in every way   
each and every day




subjects : Relationships, Pets, Nature, Animals
