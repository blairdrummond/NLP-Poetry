"A kiss on the forehead" 
Marina Tsvetaeva 
1892-1941

Russia
Love, Romantic Love

Free Verse

A kiss on the forehead--erases misery.
I kiss your forehead.

A kiss on the eyes--lifts sleeplessness.
I kiss your eyes.

A kiss on the lips--is a drink of water.
I kiss your lips.

A kiss on the forehead--erases memory.


1917
