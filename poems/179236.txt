title : Acts of Love

author : Pam Rehm

text : 
If endear is earned 
and is meant to identify   
two halves 

then it composes   
one meaning 

which means   
a token 

a knot   
a note 

a noting in the head   
of how it feels 

to have your heart   
be the dear one




subjects : Relationships, Love, Romantic Love
