title : Flannel

author : Kristine O'Connell George

text : 
Red and blue plaid,
 pockets with buttons,
 my camping shirt
 is flannel, worn soft,
 pine smells, campfire,
 forest moss.
  
 I keep it hidden
 in my bottom drawer—
 where no one will find it
 and wash away
 my memories.
 ﻿
