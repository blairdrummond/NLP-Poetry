title : A Start

author : Elaine Equi

text : 
The 
    silver 
          hour 

drops— 

a spider 
on the mirror. 

          * 

Silver 
the hour 

like drops 
of a spider’s 
mirror. 

          * 

The silver drops, 
the spider’s hour, 
the mirror . . .
