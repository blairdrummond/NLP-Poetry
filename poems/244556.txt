title : [I Saw Myself]

author : Lew Welch

text : 
I saw myself
 a ring of bone
 in the clear stream
 of all of it
  
 and vowed,
 always to be open to it
 that all of it
 might flow through
  
 and then heard
 “ring of bone” where
 ring is what a
  
 bell does




subjects : Living, The Body, The Mind, Religion, The Spiritual
