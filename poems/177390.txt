title : Catch

author : Langston Hughes

text : 
Big Boy came 
Carrying a mermaid   
On his shoulders   
And the mermaid   
Had her tail 
Curved 
Beneath his arm. 

Being a fisher boy, 
He’d found a fish 
To carry— 
Half fish,   
Half girl   
To marry.




subjects : Relationships, Men & Women, Mythology & Folklore, Fairy-tales & Legends
