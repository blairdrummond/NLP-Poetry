title : A Marriage

author : Robert Creeley

text : 
The first retainer   
he gave to her   
was a golden 
wedding ring. 

The second—late at night   
he woke up, 
leaned over on an elbow,   
and kissed her. 

The third and the last— 
he died with 
and gave up loving 
and lived with her.
