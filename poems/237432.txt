title : The Lyric In A Time of War

author : Eloise Klein Healy

text : 
for Sappho
Let my music be found wanting
in comparison
to yours (as it must)

let me be found loving
(as you were)
extravagantly the beautiful

let me find you
and the song (forever)
between us

in these terrible times
