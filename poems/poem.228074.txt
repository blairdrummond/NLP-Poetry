Ebb
Edna St. Vincent Millay
1892-1950

U.S., New, England
Living, Love, Relationships, Sorrow & Grieving, Infatuation & Crushes, Unrequited Love, Break-ups & Vexed Love, Heartache & Loss
Poems for Weddings

Epigram

I know what my heart is like 
      Since your love died: 
It is like a hollow ledge 
Holding a little pool 
      Left there by the tide, 
      A little tepid pool, 
Drying inward from the edge.
