title : Poem Without an End

author : Yehuda Amichai

text : 
Inside the brand-new museum
there’s an old synagogue.
Inside the synagogue
is me.
Inside me
my heart.
Inside my heart
a museum.
Inside the museum
a synagogue,
inside it
me,
inside me
my heart,
inside my heart
a museum


 




subjects : Religion, Judaism, Arts & Sciences, Architecture & Design
