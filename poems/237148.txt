title : [dear dusty moth]

author : Robin Blaser

text : 
dear dusty moth
wearing miller’s cloth,
Sophia Nichols’ soft
voice calls wings
at dusk
           across railroads
and sagebrush
to lull me to sleep,
‘Come to these window corners,
come, rest on my boy’s dreams
and flight,
come tonight’

                                                2 September 2002




subjects : Living, Nature, Animals, The Body
