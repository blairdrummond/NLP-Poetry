title : The Measure

author : Robert Creeley

text : 
I cannot 
move backward   
or forward. 
I am caught 

in the time 
as measure. 
What we think   
of we think of— 

of no other reason   
we think than 
just to think— 
each for himself.




subjects : Arts & Sciences, Time & Brevity, Living, Poetry & Poets, The Mind
