title : [It’s no use / Mother dear...]

author : Sappho

text : 
It’s no use
  
 Mother dear, I
 can’t finish my
 weaving
             You may
 blame Aphrodite
  
 soft as she is
  
 she has almost
 killed me with
 love for that boy
