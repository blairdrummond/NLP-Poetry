The Great Figure 
William Carlos Williams 
1883-1963
Imagist
U.S., Mid-Atlantic
Social Commentaries, Cities & Urban Life

Free Verse

Among the rain 
and lights 
I saw the figure 5 
in gold 
on a red 
firetruck 
moving 
tense 
unheeded 
to gong clangs 
siren howls 
and wheels rumbling 
through the dark city.
