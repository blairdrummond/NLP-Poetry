Wild nights - Wild nights! (269)
Emily Dickinson
1830-1886

U.S., New, England
Relationships, Love, Romantic Love, Desire, Infatuation & Crushes
Engagement, Poems for Weddings
Valentine's Day
Rhymed Stanza, Metaphor

Wild nights - Wild nights! 
Were I with thee 
Wild nights should be 
Our luxury! 

Futile - the winds - 
To a Heart in port - 
Done with the Compass - 
Done with the Chart! 

Rowing in Eden - 
Ah - the Sea! 
Might I but moor - tonight - 
In thee!
