title : Addiction

author : Ken Mikolowski

text : 
Fighting a losing battle

lives next door
to a vibrant woman
in her 30’s.
When he talks to her
sub-mediocre takes over
in a big way.
Zombie-ized by the big eye
she even sleeps with it on.
Just sign me: concerned.
