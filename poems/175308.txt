title : Warm Summer Sun

author : Mark Twain

text : 

Warm summer sun,
    Shine kindly here,
Warm southern wind,
    Blow softly here.
Green sod above,
    Lie light, lie light.
Good night, dear heart,
    Good night, good night.




subjects : Living, Summer, Nature, Marriage & Companionship, Sorrow & Grieving, Death
