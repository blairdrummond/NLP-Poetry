Oh, black Persian cat! 
Was not your life 
already cursed with offspring? 
We took you for rest to that old 
Yankee farm, -- so lonely 
and with so many field mice 
in the long grass -- 
and you return to us 
in this condition --! 

Oh, black Persian cat.
