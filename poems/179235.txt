title : White Rose

author : Tom Pickard

text : 
you gave me a white rose   
put the lamp on the stove   
it caught fire 
the I Ching said 
thunder above the lake 
lightning in Baker Street 

switched on the cooker   
and blew a fuse 
blue flash 
you see 
the whole experience   
is electric




subjects : Love, Relationships, Romantic Love
