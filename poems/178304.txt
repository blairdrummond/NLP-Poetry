title : Mating Saliva

author : Richard Brautigan

text : 
A girl in a green mini- 
skirt, not very pretty, walks   
   down the street. 

A businessman stops, turns   
to stare at her ass 
that looks like a moldy   
   refrigerator. 

There are now 200,000,000 people   
   in America.
