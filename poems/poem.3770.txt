Fabliau of Florida
Wallace Stevens
1879-1955
Modern

Nature, Stars, Planets, Heavens, Seas, Rivers, & Streams, Arts & Sciences, Language & Linguistics



Barque of phosphor
 On the palmy beach,
  
 Move outward into heaven,
 Into the alabasters
 And night blues.
  
 Foam and cloud are one.
 Sultry moon-monster
 Are dissolving.
  
 Fill your black hull
 With white moonlight.
  
 There will never be an end
 To this droning of the surf.
