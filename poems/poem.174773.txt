Winter Trees
William Carlos Williams
1883-1963
Imagist
U.S., Mid-Atlantic
Trees & Flowers, Winter, Nature, Landscapes & Pastorals
Poems for Weddings

Free Verse

All the complicated details 
of the attiring and 
the disattiring are completed! 
A liquid moon 
moves gently among 
the long branches. 
Thus having prepared their buds 
against a sure winter 
the wise trees 
stand sleeping in the cold.
