1881-1958

Spain

Poems for Weddings

Free Verse

They all are asleep, below.   
                                        Above, awake,   
the helmsman and I. 

   He, watching the compass needle, lord 
of the bodies, with their keys turned 
in the locks. I, with my eyes 
toward the infinite, guiding 
the open treasures of the souls.
