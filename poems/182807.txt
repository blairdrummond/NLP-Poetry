title : Come slowly – Eden! (205)

author : Emily Dickinson

text : 
Come slowly – Eden! 
Lips unused to Thee – 
Bashful – sip thy Jessamines – 
As the fainting Bee – 

Reaching late his flower, 
Round her chamber hums – 
Counts his nectars – 
Enters – and is lost in Balms. 






subjects : Nature, Religion, Living
