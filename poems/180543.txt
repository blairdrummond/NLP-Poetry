title : Repetition

author : Kay Ryan

text : 
Trying to walk 
the same way 
to the same store 
takes high-wire 
balance: 
each step 
not exactly 
as before 
risks chasms 
of flatness. 
One stumble 
alone and   
nothing 
happens. 
Few are 
the willing 
and fewer 
the champions.
