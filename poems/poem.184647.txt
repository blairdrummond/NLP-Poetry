Parks and ponds 
Ralph Waldo Emerson 
1803-1882

U.S., New, England
Nature, Activities, Travels & Journeys, Landscapes & Pastorals, Arts & Sciences, Philosophy, Social Commentaries, Town & Country Life

Aphorism

Parks and ponds are good by day; 
I do not delight 
In black acres of the night, 
Nor my unseasoned step disturbs 
The sleeps of trees or dreams of herbs.
