Song ["Only the wanderer"]
Ivor Gurney
1890-1937
Modern
England
Nature, Landscapes & Pastorals, Mythology & Folklore, Heroes & Patriotism
Poems for Weddings

Quatrain, Rhymed Stanza

Only the wanderer
   Knows England's graces,
Or can anew see clear
   Familiar faces.

And who loves joy as he
   That dwells in shadows?
Do not forget me quite,
   O Severn meadows.
