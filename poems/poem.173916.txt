There was a little girl 
Henry Wadsworth Longfellow 
1807-1882
Victorian
U.S., New, England
Living, Youth, Infancy

Epigram

There was a little girl, 
            Who had a little curl, 
Right in the middle of her forehead. 
            When she was good, 
            She was very good indeed, 
But when she was bad she was horrid.
