Infant Joy
William Blake
1757-1827
Romantic
England
Living, Infancy
Birth, Birthdays, Poems for Weddings

Refrain

I have no name 
I am but two days old.-- 
What shall I call thee? 
I happy am 
Joy is my name,-- 
Sweet joy befall thee! 

Pretty joy! 
Sweet joy but two days old, 
Sweet joy I call thee; 
Thou dost smile. 
I sing the while 
Sweet joy befall thee.
