title : Afternoons

author : Michael Anania

text : 
Quick passage into   
memory and behind   
only blank spaces, 

blue stain on pink   
litmus or merely   
known so closely 

something falls away   
receding from touch,   
caught in the air 

your fingers move,   
agile water-fly   
padding the surface 

of what is seen   
even among these   
defractions, bent 

pencil or warps   
of a flat eye, 
the wide world circling.




subjects : Sciences, Nature, Relationships, Arts & Sciences
