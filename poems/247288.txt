title : The Poets light but Lamps — (930)

author : Emily Dickinson

text : 
The Poets light but Lamps —
Themselves — go out —
The Wicks they stimulate
If vital Light

Inhere as do the Suns —
Each Age a Lens
Disseminating their
Circumference —




subjects : Living, Death, Nature, Stars, Planets, Heavens
