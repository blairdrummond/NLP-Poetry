title : December 26

author : Kenn Nesbitt

text : 
A BB gun. 
A model plane. 
A basketball. 
A ’lectric train. 
A bicycle. 
A cowboy hat. 
A comic book. 
A baseball bat. 
A deck of cards. 
A science kit. 
A racing car. 
A catcher’s mitt. 
So that’s my list 
of everything 
that Santa Claus 
forgot to bring.
