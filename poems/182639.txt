title : Change

author : Wendy Videlock

text : 
Change is the new, 

improved 

word for god, 

lovely enough 
to raise a song 

or implicate 

a sea of wrongs, 
mighty enough, 

like other gods, 

to shelter, 
bring together, 

and estrange us. 

Please, god, 
we seem to say, 

change us.




subjects : Religion, God & the Divine, The Spiritual
