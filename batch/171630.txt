title : “A bridge engineer, Mr. Crumpett ...”

author : Anonymous

text : 
A bridge engineer, Mr. Crumpett, 
Built a bridge for the good River Bumpett. 
    A mistake in the plan 
    Left a gap in the span, 
But he said, “Well, they'll just have to jump it.”
